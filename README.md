# 👥 HR Employee Attrition Analysis

> An exploratory data analysis (EDA) project on IBM's HR Employee Attrition dataset,
> uncovering the key factors that drive employees to leave their organization.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most costly challenges for any organization.
This project analyzes **1,470 employee records** to identify patterns and risk factors
associated with employees leaving — providing actionable insights for HR decision-makers.

**Tools & Libraries:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

| # | Question |
|---|----------|
| 1 | What is the overall attrition rate in the company? |
| 2 | Does working overtime increase the chance of leaving? |
| 3 | Which department has the highest attrition rate? |
| 4 | How is monthly income distributed across departments and job roles? |


---

## 📊 Key Findings

- **16.1% attrition rate** — 237 out of 1,470 employees left the company.
- Employees who work **overtime are ~30% more likely to leave** than those who don't.
- **Sales** has a higher average income but also a higher attrition rate vs. R&D.
